Biography

Manuele Leonelli is an Assistant Professor of Statistics at the School of Human Sciences and Technology, IE University, Madrid. His research interests include probabilistic graphical models, sensitivity analysis and inference for extreme events.

Before moving to Madrid, he was a Lecturer in the School of Mathematics and Statistics at the University of Glasgow and a Visiting Professor in the School of Medicine at McGill University. Before that, he was a CAPES postdoctoral fellow at the Federal University of Rio de Jainero.

He is currently the editor of Bernoulli News.
