Manuele Leonelli

Assistant Professor of Statistics

School of Human Sciences and Technology, IE University

Manuele Leonelli is an Assistant Professor of Statistics at the School of Human Sciences and Technology, IE University. His research interests include probabilistic graphical models, sensitivity analysis and inference for exteme events. He is currently the editor of Bernoulli News.
